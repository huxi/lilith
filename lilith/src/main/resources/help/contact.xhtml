Contact

You have several options to stay in touch with the further development of Lilith.

Use the Wiki. It also contains the issue tracker you may use to request new features or report bugs. A sourceforge.net account is required.

You can subscribe to one of the mailinglists. You can subscribe to them from the Lilith project page.

You may also follow Lilith on Twitter if you like.

Lastly, you can simply write me a mail.
